The Leadership Centre is accredited by the International Association for Continuing Education and Training (IACET). The Leadership Centre complies with the ANSI/IACET Standard, which is recognized internationally as a standard of excellence in instructional practices. As a result of this accreditation, The Leadership Centre is authorized to issue the IACET CEU.

As an IACET Accredited Provider, The Leadership Centre offers CEUs for its programs that qualify under the ANSI/IACET Standard.
